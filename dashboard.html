🎯 KPI Priority Guide — What to watch & when
⬤ TIER 1 — Weekly Watch
MER (Marketing Efficiency Ratio)
Revenue / MKT Spend. Target ≥ 10×. Drop >15% = immediate action.
Revenue vs Plan
Actual vs FC1 by vertical. Alert if <90% of plan by mid-month.
⬤ TIER 2 — Monthly Review
LTV / CAC Ratio
Healthy = LTV/CAC ≥ 3×. Based on 2.5M lifespan cohort data.
Cohort M1 Retention
MKT-attributed new users. Benchmark: ~55–65% for PTY/JOB/VEH.
App NURR M1
% new app users retained after 1 month. Benchmark: 6.8–7.3%.
⬤ TIER 3 — Quarterly Strategic
vMAU Growth Trend
Vertical MAU — leading indicator for revenue sustainability.
Direct Channel Share
App direct ~55% acquisition → measures organic brand pull.
New App MAU Trend
Monthly new users. Peak Mar 345K → Watch for seasonality dips.

Traffic Channels & P&L Impact

Role of each channel in the cost structure

PAID — MUST PROVE ROI
paid search High intent. Highest CAC, matching LTV. Best for PTY & VEH.
display Awareness + retargeting. Mid CAC.
growth-outapp App installs. Measure CAC per Activated User, not raw install.
Chain: Brand Reach↑ → Brand Clicks↑ → Direct↑ → CAC↓ → MER↑
INDIRECT / ORGANIC — COMPOUNDING RETURNS
organic search SEO. Near-zero CAC once ranked. Traffic visible monthly; ROI lags 3–6 months.
direct Brand Equity + Product stickiness. When direct grows → Blended CAC falls → MER improves.
growth-inapp In-app cross-sell, push notification, CRM. Lowest CAC — retention-driven.
social / referral Brand-driven virality. High quality users, hard to scale predictably.
