🎯 KPI Priority Guide — What to watch & when
⬤ TIER 1 — Weekly Watch
MER (Marketing Efficiency Ratio)
Revenue / MKT Spend. Target ≥ 10×. Drop >15% = immediate action.
Revenue vs Plan
Actual vs FC1 by vertical. Alert if <90% of plan by mid-month.
⬤ TIER 2 — Monthly Review
LTV / CAC Ratio
Healthy = LTV/CAC ≥ 3×. Based on 2.5M lifespan cohort data.
Cohort M1 Retention
MKT-attributed new users. Benchmark: ~55–65% for PTY/JOB/VEH.
App NURR M1
% new app users retained after 1 month. Benchmark: 6.8–7.3%.
⬤ TIER 3 — Quarterly Strategic
vMAU Growth Trend
Vertical MAU — leading indicator for revenue sustainability.
Direct Channel Share
App direct ~55% acquisition → measures organic brand pull.
New App MAU Trend
Monthly new users. Peak Mar 345K → Watch for seasonality dips.
